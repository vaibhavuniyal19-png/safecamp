Flood Safety

Learn water safety and flood preparedness techniques

Before a Flood

Preparation Steps

  • Know your area's flood risk and evacuation routes
  • Keep sandbags and emergency supplies ready
  • Install sump pumps and backup power
  • Review flood insurance coverage

Warning Signs

  • Heavy rainfall for extended periods
  • Rising water levels in nearby rivers
  • Dam or levee failure warnings
  • Flash flood watches and warnings

During a Flood

Safety Rules

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Never walk through moving water
  • Turn around, don't drown - avoid flooded roads
  • Stay informed via weather radio

After a Flood

Safety Precautions

  • Avoid electrical hazards and downed power lines
  • Don't drink contaminated water
  • Inspect home for structural damage
  • Document damage for insurance claims

Cleanup Process

  • Wear protective gear during cleanup
  • Ventilate buildings to prevent mold
  • Discard contaminated items safely
  • Disinfect all surfaces thoroughly
