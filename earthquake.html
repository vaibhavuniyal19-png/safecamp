Earthquake Safety

Learn essential earthquake preparedness and response techniques

Before an Earthquake

Preparation Steps

  • Create an emergency kit with water, food, and supplies
  • Secure heavy furniture and appliances to walls
  • Identify safe spots in each room (under sturdy tables)
  • Practice earthquake drills with family

Emergency Kit Essentials

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day for 3 days)
  • Non-perishable food for 3 days
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • First aid kit and medications

During an Earthquake

DROP

Drop to your hands and knees immediately

COVER

Take cover under a sturdy desk or table

HOLD ON

Hold on to your shelter and protect your head

After an Earthquake

Immediate Actions

  • Check for injuries and provide first aid
  • Inspect your home for damage
  • Listen to emergency broadcasts

Safety Precautions

  • Be prepared for aftershocks
  • Check for gas leaks and electrical damage
  • Use phone only for emergencies
